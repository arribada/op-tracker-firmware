11 /**
12  * @page kns_rf_page KNS_RF DRIVER
13  *
14  * This page is presenting the generic RF driver APIs for communication with
15  * radio HW (\ref KNS_RF). This is a common abstraction layer to any RF chip used for Kineis.
16  *
17  * Depending on the HW platform (UL-only KIM1, UL/DL KIM2-x), this abstraction layer will forward
18  * actions to the corresponding low-level-RF drivers.
19  *
20  * This driver provides functions to send or receive data to/from Kineis Transceiver.
21  *
22  * \note As our system requires some RF POWER for correct transmission to the SATS, it may be
23  * usefull to driver an external PA through an external GPIO. According to its implementation
24  * an integrator can tune its GPIOS through the MCU misc wrapper.
25  *
26  * @section kns_app_subpages Sub-pages
27  *
28  * * @subpage mcu_misc_page
29  *
30  */
